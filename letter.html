Exotic Dance moves

Yes, you're right! As part of my post-doctorate, i did study exotic tribal dances. Definitions:

Polynesian chicken dances
A little known but very influential dance dating back as far as 300BC, a whoe village would dance around in a circle like chickens, to encourage or be "fruitful"
Icelandic brownian shuffle
Before the Icelander developed fire as means of getting warm, they used to pracetice this dance, which involved hudding close together in a circle on the floor.
Artic robot dance
An interesting example of historic misinformation, English explorers int the 1960s beleived to have discovered a new dance style by "robotic", stilted movements, being practiced by inhabitants of Northern Alaska and Canada.
